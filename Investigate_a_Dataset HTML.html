Questions answered in this course:¶

  1. What is the relation between the budget and profit?

Conclusions¶

From the previous data analysis, I came out with this conclusions:

  1. High budget leads to high profit.
  2. The higher the budget, the higher the profit potential.
  3. Ranking of movies based on highest profit (Avatar, Star Wars: The Force Awakens, Titanic, Jurassic World, Furious 7)
  4. Between 2011 and 2015, there was a severe decrease in the number of released movies.
  5. Arrangement of years according to the number of movies released (2011, 2015, 2012, 2013, 2014)

Limitations¶

  • In parsing the tmdb-movies dataset, the data is not up-to-date, it is also incomplete and has missing values.
  • The currency unit is not provided, so I assume it's the same unit, which can lead to a huge error in data analysis.
